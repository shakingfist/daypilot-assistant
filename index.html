DayPilot Assistant

DayPilot Assistant is a smart task manager designed to help you organize your day with categories, swipe gestures, and Siri voice commands.

Key Features

  • Create and organize tasks by category
  • Swipe right to mark tasks complete
  • Swipe left to delete tasks
  • Add tasks and notes using Siri
  • Optional reminders and due dates
  • iCloud sync using Apple CloudKit

Siri & Voice Support

You can use Siri to interact with DayPilot Assistant.

  • “Add a task in DayPilot Assistant”
  • “Create a to-do in DayPilot Assistant”
  • “Add a note to my task in DayPilot Assistant”

Privacy Policy

DayPilot Assistant respects your privacy. Your tasks are stored securely on your device and synced using Apple iCloud (CloudKit) when enabled. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Voice input is processed locally or by Apple services as part of Siri and dictation functionality. DayPilot Assistant does not store or transmit voice recordings.
